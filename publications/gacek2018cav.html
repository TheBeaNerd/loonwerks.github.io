--- layout: publication authors: [A. Gacek, J. Backes, M. Whalen, L. Wagner, E. Ghassabani] title: "The JKind Model Checker" info: "Computer Aided Verification 2018" link: "http://arxiv.org/pdf/1712.01222" projects: [HACMS] tools: [JKind, AGREE, SpeAR] date: 2018-07-14 00:00:00 --- JKind is an open-source industrial model checker developed by Rockwell Collins and the University of Minnesota. JKind uses multiple parallel engines to prove or falsify safety properties of infinite state models. It is portable, easy to install, performance competitive with other state-of-the-art model checkers, and has features designed to improve the results presented to users: inductive validity cores for proofs and counterexample smoothing for test-case generation. It serves as the back-end for various industrial applications.
