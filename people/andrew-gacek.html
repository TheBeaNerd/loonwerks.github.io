Gacek position: Senior Industrial Logician email: andrew.gacek@gmail.com picture: /images/lorem.gif --- Andrew Gacek is a Senior Industrial Logician at the Rockwell Collins Advanced Technology Center. He earned his PhD in Computer Science from the University of Minnesota. He is the principle developer of JKind, a k-induction model checker used in various projects within Rockwell Collins. He is currently working as part of the air vehicle team in DARPA's High Assurance Cyber Military Systems project, focusing high-level structural and behavioral properties for unmanned air vehicles. His interests include theorem proving, model checking, language translation, and software design.
